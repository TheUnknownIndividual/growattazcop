Germany's Solar Revolution: Solar Package 1 Unleashes New Opportunities

Germany's Solar Industry Reaches Historic Milestone

Germany's solar industry has reached a historic turning point with the unanimous approval of the Bundestag and Bundesrat for the implementation of the Solar Package 1 at the end of April. This decisive step marks a significant milestone for Germany in achieving its solar expansion and climate protection goals.

Simplifying processes, promoting balcony solar installations, and supporting commercial and tenant solar systems, the Solar Package 1 opens new horizons for the popularization and application of solar energy in Germany.

The Rise of Balcony Solar Power Systems

Since April 1, the Federal Network Agency has greatly simplified the registration process for solar power systems, requiring only basic data for easy registration without cumbersome preliminary procedures. Additionally, the law has reasonably relaxed the installed capacity limits for photovoltaic (PV) modules and inverters, allowing a total installed capacity of up to 2 kilowatts for PV modules and 800 watts for inverters. This provides ample space for efficient energy utilization.

A New Era for Public Buildings and Tenant Electricity

The introduction of the "public building supply" mechanism by the Solar Package 1 provides apartment tenants with the convenience of directly using solar energy from rooftops, garages, or battery storage systems. Growatt's comprehensive solar solutions, including photovoltaic inverters, energy storage systems, and EV charging stations, can meet the complex needs of apartment buildings, ensuring tenants can enjoy economical and clean energy, whether for residential or commercial purposes.

New Opportunities for Commercial and Agricultural Solar Power

The Solar Package 1 has removed barriers to the development of commercial solar power systems and agrivoltaics. Systems exceeding 100 kilowatts can now be exempt from direct marketing costs, while streamlining the process of transferring surplus energy to grid operators, making it more feasible to install large-scale photovoltaic systems on commercial rooftops.

Growatt's Comprehensive Solar Solutions

With its cutting-edge technology and innovative product offerings, Growatt is poised to play a pivotal role in supporting the widespread deployment of solar power systems across Germany. From residential rooftops to commercial and industrial facilities, Growatt's comprehensive range of solutions, including high-efficiency inverters, intelligent energy storage systems, and integrated EV charging stations, provide seamless integration and optimal energy management.

Balcony Solar Power Systems

Growatt's NEO series microinverters, featuring 2 MPPTs, IP67 protection, and integrated Wi-Fi communication, become the perfect choice for balcony solar systems in Germany. These microinverters significantly improve power generation efficiency. When paired with the Noah balcony energy storage system, even during the cold winter months, it can ensure a stable supply of 8 kWh and over 6000 cycles of use, providing uninterrupted clean energy throughout the year.

Commercial and Agricultural Solar Power

Growatt's MAX series commercial inverters, with up to 99% conversion efficiency and intelligent diagnostic functions, provide strong support for energy transformation for commercial solar users. These advanced inverters ensure optimal performance, efficient energy conversion, and seamless integration, enabling businesses and farmers to harness the full potential of solar power while contributing to Germany's sustainable energy goals.

Growatt Leads the Charge Towards a Sustainable Future

Growatt's commitment to sustainability extends beyond its products, as the company actively fosters partnerships and collaborations with industry stakeholders, policymakers, and local communities. By working hand in hand with customers, Growatt aims to promote a holistic approach to energy transformation, addressing not only the technical aspects but also the social and environmental implications of this transition.

As Germany embraces the Solar Package 1 and its potential for accelerating the adoption of renewable energy sources, Growatt stands ready to be a trusted partner, providing innovative solutions, expert guidance, and unwavering support. Together, they will pave the way for a sustainable future, where clean energy is not just a goal but a reality that benefits generations to come.
